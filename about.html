About Northmanger

We exist because the EU–Africa fintech corridor is one of the most valuable — and most punishing — expansion pathways in global financial services. Operators keep failing for the same avoidable reasons. We built the infrastructure to change that.

Built for the Corridor Nobody Has Fully Mapped

Northmanger is a market entry and growth intelligence firm for fintech operators and financial institutions expanding across the EU–Africa corridor. We build decision infrastructure — simulation engines, regulatory maps, and expansion architectures — that clients own and operate long after our engagement ends.

We are not a consulting firm that sells time and opinions. Every Northmanger engagement produces a working system: a model you can run, a framework your team can execute against, a database you can query when the market shifts. The difficulty of this corridor is our moat — and your competitive advantage when you navigate it correctly.

The Principles We Work By

These commitments shape every engagement, every model, and every piece of intelligence we produce.

Infrastructure Over Opinions

We don't hand you a recommendation and walk away. We build systems — models, frameworks, and databases — that remain useful after the engagement ends.

Priced Against Failure

A failed market entry costs $300K–$1M or more. We price our work against the cost of getting it wrong — not the cost of our time.

Native Corridor Knowledge

Our intelligence is grounded in real understanding of how regulation, infrastructure, and market culture interact across Nigeria, Kenya, Ghana, South Africa, the UK, and the EU.

Defensible, Data-Backed Work

Intelligence Sources & Data Partners
